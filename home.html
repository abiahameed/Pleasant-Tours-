Tour Itineraries

Detailed day-by-day plans for our popular tours

Tropical Paradise - 7 Days

Day 1: Arrival at island airport and transfer to resort. Evening welcome dinner by the beach.

Day 2: Morning: Beach relaxation and complimentary water sports (kayaking, paddleboarding). Afternoon: Sunset view from the infinity pool.

Day 3: Full-day island hopping tour, including three different snorkeling spots and a secluded picnic lunch.

Day 4: Cultural village tour on a nearby island, followed by a hands-on local cuisine cooking experience.

Day 5: Free day for personal exploration, optional scuba diving lesson, or spa treatment.

Day 6: Romantic sunset cruise with cocktails and a special farewell dinner.

Day 7: Breakfast and departure to the airport.

Mountain Escape - 5 Days

Day 1: Arrival at mountain base and scenic transfer to chalet accommodation with panoramic views. Orientation session.

Day 2: Guided 4-hour hike through the famous 'Whispering Trails.' Lunch at a high-altitude tea house.

Day 3: Morning: Visit to local artisan markets and the historic town square. Afternoon: Free time for photography or relaxation.

Day 4: Adventure activities: Choose between white-water rafting (seasonal) or a cable car ride to the highest accessible peak.

Day 5: Breakfast and departure with a packed gourmet lunch for the road.

Royal Heritage - 6 Days

Day 1: Arrival in the capital city. Transfer to a heritage hotel.

Day 2: Full-day exploration of the main **Fortress** with a certified historian guide, covering defensive walls and royal quarters.

Day 3: Visit the opulent **Grand Palace**, including the private rooms and the famous Hall of Mirrors. Afternoon tour of the Royal Gardens.

Day 4: Morning: Visits to the city's key museums detailing the local dynasty's history. Evening: Traditional cultural performance.

Day 5: Hands-on workshop on traditional craft (e.g., miniature painting or pottery). Farewell dinner in a palace courtyard.

Day 6: Breakfast and departure.

Cultural Immersion - 8 Days

Day 1: Arrival and transfer to a local family-run homestay for authentic experience.

Day 2: Guided walking tour of the old city's hidden lanes and temples. Evening: Traditional cooking class.

Day 3: Day trip to nearby artisan villages known for weaving and woodwork.

Day 4-5: Participation in a local community festival or market day.

Day 6: Exploration of local farming practices and nature reserves.

Day 7: Reflective free morning. Afternoon: Farewell tea ceremony with the host family.

Day 8: Departure.

Download Full Itinerary (PDF)

Coastal Retreat - 7 Days

Day 1: Arrival at coastal resort. Free time to settle in and enjoy the private beach area.

Day 2: Morning yoga session by the sea. Guided snorkeling tour of the coral reefs.

Day 3: Sailing excursion to a remote bay for swimming and sunbathing.

Day 4: Relaxation day: optional spa treatment or explore the nearby town at your leisure.

Day 5: Sunset fishing trip with a fresh seafood dinner prepared by the crew.

Day 6: Visit to a marine conservation center to learn about local wildlife.

Day 7: Departure.

Download Full Itinerary (PDF)

Alpine Adventure - 6 Days

Day 1: Arrival in the Alpine town. Check-in to a traditional lodge.

Day 2: Guided climb or via ferrata experience (depending on skill level).

Day 3: Full day dedicated to exploring high-altitude lakes and valleys by mountain bike (bikes provided).

Day 4: Visit the local cheese and chocolate factories. Learn about the region's culinary heritage.

Day 5: Leisure day or optional paragliding/ziplining activity for adrenaline seekers.

Day 6: Breakfast and departure.
